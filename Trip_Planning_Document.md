## Fare Types

- **Promo:** Cheapest, non-refundable, non-changeable (book early!)
- **Promo+:** Slightly more, some flexibility
- **Flexible:** Most expensive, fully refundable/changeable

## Booking Tips

- Book 60-90 days in advance for best Promo prices
- Check for group discounts (6+ people)
- Children under 4 usually free, 4-13 often discounted
- Select window seats for scenic views
- Download tickets to phone (QR codes)

## Boarding Process

1. Arrive at station 15-30 minutes before departure
2. Check departure board for platform (and√©n)
3. Go to platform (no security check needed)
4. Show QR code to conductor when boarding
5. Find your assigned seat

## Luggage

- Carry-on: Small bags can go overhead or under seat
- Large bags: Storage areas at end of cars
- No weight limits, but be reasonable
